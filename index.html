Academic Interests

My academic interests include geographic data science, census analysis, urban demographics, cartography, spatial modeling, and ethical uses of data. Through GEOG 490, I have learned how to use R, census data, maps, and spatial methods to describe patterns of population, race/ethnicity, income, segregation, and urban change.

About This Website

This website was created as a final portfolio for GEOG 490/590. The portfolio tells a case study about the Seattle-Tacoma-Bellevue metropolitan area using demographic maps, census variables, spatial analysis, and written interpretation.
